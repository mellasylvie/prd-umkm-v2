# **App Name**: UMKM Boost

## Core Features:

- Minisite Builder: Enable UMKM to create a simple, elegant minisite with a drag-and-drop interface and pre-designed templates.
- Product Catalog: Allow UMKM to create and manage a product catalog with images, descriptions, prices, and stock status.
- Automated Promotion: Generate promotional content, including captions and posters, using AI based on product details and templates. This feature should act as a tool, deciding what product details to incorporate in the output.
- Visitor Insights: Provide UMKM with insights into visitor activity, including total visits, popular products, and CTA clicks.
- Template Switcher: Offer a selection of professionally designed templates for minisites and promotional posters.
- Integrated Sharing: Allow UMKM to easily share promotional content directly to WhatsApp and Instagram.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft gold (#D4AF37) to evoke a sense of premium quality and elegance.
- Background color: Charcoal gray (#1A1A1A) to provide a modern and sophisticated backdrop.
- Accent color: Blue-gold gradient, subtly applied to buttons and highlights, to draw the eye without overwhelming the interface.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif with a neutral and modern appearance that works well for both headings and body text.
- Employ clean spacing and generous whitespace to create a sense of openness and sophistication, mirroring the minimalist aesthetic of Airtable and Notion.
- Use modern line icons from Feather Icons or Phosphor Icons to maintain a consistent and refined visual language.
- Incorporate subtle animations for transitions and micro-interactions to enhance the user experience, ensuring they are smooth and do not distract from the content.
